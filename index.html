FAQ

Good questions

Is KarmaWheel free to use?

KarmaWheel keeps platform fees to the absolute minimum. You connect and transact directly with your neighbor — the platform stays out of your transaction.

How local is "local"?

Jobs are surfaced based on your device location. The map view shows exactly where each job is pinned — most are walkable or a short drive from wherever you are.

What kind of jobs can I post?

Anything local and legal — pet care, gardening, home repair, errands, cleaning, tutoring, moving help. If a neighbor can do it, it belongs on KarmaWheel.

How does the karma system work?

After each job, both parties can leave a rating. Your score builds your reputation — higher karma means more visibility and trust from your community.

Is it available on Android?

iOS only right now. Android is on the roadmap — join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know when it launches.
