FAQ

Good questions

Is KarmaWheel free to use?

Completely free. KarmaWheel takes no cut from jobs — you connect and transact directly with your neighbor. The platform stays out of the transaction entirely.

How local is "local"?

Jobs are surfaced based on your device location. The map view shows exactly where each job is pinned — most are walkable distance from wherever you are.

What kind of jobs can I post?

Anything local and legal — pet care, gardening, home repair, errands, cleaning, tutoring, moving help. If a neighbor can do it, it belongs on KarmaWheel.

How does the karma system work?

After each job, both parties can leave a rating. Your score builds your reputation on the platform — higher karma means more visibility and trust from your community.

Is it available on Android?

iOS only right now. Android is on the roadmap — download on iOS and you'll be the first to know when it launches.
